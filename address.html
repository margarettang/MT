Resource Centre

The Resource Centre provides reference library service on tobacco control for the general public. The collections include books, audio-videos materials, research papers, conference proceedings, reference kits on the hazards of tobacco smoking, secondhand smoke, tobacco control legislations and development of tobacco control in recent decades of Hong Kong. Visitors of Resource Centre include students, teachers, parents, researchers and oversea delegations on public health and tobacco control, medical and health practitioners as well as the general public.
